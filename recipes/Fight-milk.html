Fight Milk!

Description

Fight Milk! is an alcoholic based protien drink made for BodyGuards by BodyGuards. Drink it every morning so you can fight like a crow.

Ingredients

  • 1 gallon (3.8 liters) whole milk
  • One (750-ml) bottle vodka
  • A dozen Crow's Egg

Directions

  1. In a giant pot crack 12 Crows egg.
  2. Add gallon of milk
  3. Mix together.
  4. While stiring, Add Vodka
  5. Pour into cup or glass bottle.
